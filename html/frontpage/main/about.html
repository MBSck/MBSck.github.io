About myself

My name is Marten Scheuck. I am years old and at the moment pursuing my PhD in astronomy at the Max-Planck-Institute of Astronomy in Heidelberg (MPIA).

It has always been my dream to contribute to contemporary science. This is why I decided on conducting research in the field of astronomy.
I am especially interested in combining up-to-date programming techniques with observational data.

A few hobbies of mine are digital drawing, coding in my freetime, and a few other things, such as playing the piano/guitar, dancing, learning languages and doing karate (空手).
