About myself

My name is Marten Scheuck. I am 25 years old and at the moment pursuing my PhD in astronomy at the Max-Planck-Institute of Astronomy in Heidelberg.

It has always been my dream to contribute to the contemporary science and do so in the field of astronomy.
I am especially interested in combining up-to-date programming techniques with observational data.

A few hobbies of mine are digital drawing, coding in my freetime, and a few other things, such as playing the piano/guitar, dancing, learning languages and doing 空手 (karate).

Personal projects

• Universum-Sim:

This was the final course project of an advanced Python class. It is an extremly simplified simulation of planetary bodies' movement. Will be updated steadily and soon made into a 3D form

• AutomatedWallpaperChanger (AWC):

The AWC program serves as a testrun for how to program on different OS. It changes the desktop's wallpaper with some additional widgets.

Discord bot that provides different functionalities in-app and is built to run continously on a server (e.g., raspberry-pi)
