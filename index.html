# About

My name is Marten B. Scheuck. I am pursuing my PhD in astronomy at the Max-Planck-Institute for Astronomy (MPIA). I am especially interested in combining up-to-date programming techniques with observational data.

# Research Interests

I study protoplanetary discs using the MATISSE instrument. The inner regions of the discs (i.e. from <1 au to a few astronomical units), where terrestrial planet-formation is likely to occurr, are particular interesting to me. The aim of my research is to understand the structure and composition of these regions. I do this by analysising interferometric data over multiple wavelength bands (H, K, L, M, and N) via Bayesian model fitting with (simple) radiative transfer codes (analytical temperature gradient).
